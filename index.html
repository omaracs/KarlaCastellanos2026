Our Story

Expertise You Can Trust

I am a licensed insurance professional and tax preparer dedicated to helping individuals, families, and businesses protect their finances and stay compliant.

I specialize in providing insurance solutions throughout California, including auto, motorcycle, commercial, renters, and homeowners coverage. My goal is to help clients find the right protection at competitive rates while making the process simple and stress-free.

In addition to insurance services, I am a licensed tax preparer serving clients in 49 states (all except New York). I assist individuals with accurate, reliable tax preparation while helping them maximize eligible deductions and credits.

To further support my clients’ needs, I also provide auto dealer bonds, tax preparer bonds, and notary bonds for professionals operating in California.

My approach is centered on personalized service, clear communication, and building long-term relationships. Whether you need insurance coverage, tax preparation, or bonding services, I am committed to providing dependable guidance and solutions you can trust.
